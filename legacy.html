LEGACY 

Today, Newgrounds still maintains a level of popularity among hardcore fans and animation enthusiasts. Tom and his team have focused on keeping up the site as well as managing his video game development studio, The Behemoth. He is a father of two children and lives in Pennsilvania, as he has all his life.

While Tom saw success through his own site, it is important to note that he has launched the careers of many in the world of internet animation. Most recently, two animators, Zach “ Psychicpebbles” Hadel and Michael Cusack, two animators that made their names on the site, recently debuted an original TV show for Adult Swim, ``Smiling Friends'', to rave reviews. Hundreds of other users have gone on to work in graphic design, audio engineering and game design, citing their tenure on Newgrounds as extremely formative.
