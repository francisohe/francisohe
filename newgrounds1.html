SITE HISTORY 

Newgrounds quickly became the home for content on the net preceding the rise of YouTube in the early 2000s. Content ranged from benign to political and shocking, with the most notivable shift being in boundary-pushing content following the 9/11 attacks in 2001. Newgrounds fostered a strong community of users that have populated the site's forum to this very day.

  Some of the site's features are mainstays in contemporary media sharing sites, most prominent was the user-posted content. Sites previously used models where videos would be sent to the webmasters who would determine who would be featured; newgrounds ended this trend with its acclaimed user-submitted content.

 Along with user submitted content, to prevent the site from being filled with garbage, Fulp utilized a rating system that would promote good content to the site and would minimize the presence of poor content, with the lowest rated material being removed or “Blammed!” from the site.
